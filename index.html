Kim's Voiceover Story

At the studio warming up my vocal chords, so I can practice my script

I like to sing becase it helps me get relax and focus on the character I am honning on becoming.

Growing up watching cartoons and seeing animals talk on commercials were different for me because I would mock their voices. I found enjoyment knowing that I could be a different person by creating a different voice. This gave me practice to change my pitch, tone, volume, and tempo.
